Ceremonies and Festivals

Ceremony of MatrimonyA three day long celebration! The first day begins with two groups for two large hunts beginning at dawn(2-6 characters per thread). The couple is separated and placed in each group as the leader. One group must hunt for the feast, the other group may choose to hunt prey, or collect resources like decorations for the party. Whoever finishes their thread first (3 rounds for each member of the group) wins a Small Accessory (of their choice!)! The first day ends with a small party for the happy couple and the winners. The second day is the ceremony! The Artifex will tie the couple's front paws together with a ribbon or chord under a natural arch of thier choice. Then the couple will speak thier vows over the tie that binds them, then kiss. After the kiss, the feast starts! Allies and friends of Norad are welcome to join the feast. It is customary to bring gifts for the couple and place them in a pile next to the entrance of their home. A bonfire is set in the middle of the village where the revelers can partake in drinking, singing, toasts, feasting, dancing, and stories together well into the third day. The last of the guests to wake the next day is said to be cursed with bad luck for three days! So rise and shine when you can!
New life celebrationa celebration to honor the new life brought into the world! It usually takes place a week before a female is due. Games are played, such as a guessing game to guess how many babies will be born. No alcohol will be served here, and males will typically be shooed off. A small lunch is usually served before it's ended. Allies and friends may attend by invitation of the mother-to-be. Gifts are not customary but are welcome for the soon-to-be mother.
Craft faira market style festival held every summer in the village, all are welcome to display wares in a stall and buy and trade wares from others. Allies and friends are welcome to attend as well as to have their own stall set up. There are usually food stalls and drink stalls as well. The Artifex welcomes and encourages wolves of Norad to display their wares and crafts for our allies to peruse and barter for. Stalls are limited to a large pelts. Depending on how the year has been there may even be excess livestock for trade at the Artifex's home.
