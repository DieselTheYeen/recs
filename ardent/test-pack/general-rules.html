• Norad welcomes all, regardless of lineage, history, or alignment.
    We would like to see one post a week from our members at least!

  • The word of the Artifex is law. They have the final say in the pack.

  • Treat your fellow pack members with respect, regardless of differences.
    You may not all get along, but respect must be upheld even as you aim to settle differences with a fight.

  • Harming or maiming a fellow pack member, ally, or guest is grounds for treason.
    Treasonous actions will be met with either expulsion or, in very rare cases, imprisonment.

  • Reproduction is not allowed without the consent of the Artifex.
    Please take care to reproduce responsibly so we can keep an eye on our population limit.
    Reproduction Persmissions: please DM Asena on discord to ask for permission

  • Do not cause trouble with other packs.
    You are responsible for your behavior, and the Artifex will not save you from the consequences of your actions. Depending on the level of trouble, it might even be grounds for treason.
