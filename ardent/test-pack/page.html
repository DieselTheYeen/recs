General Rules

  • Norad welcomes all, regardless of lineage, history, or alignment.
    We would like to see one post a week from our members at least!

  • The word of the Artifex is law. They have the final say in the pack.

  • Treat your fellow pack members with respect, regardless of differences.
    You may not all get along, but respect must be upheld even as you aim to settle differences with a fight.

  • Harming or maiming a fellow pack member, ally, or guest is grounds for treason.
    Treasonous actions will be met with either expulsion or, in very rare cases, imprisonment.

  • Reproduction is not allowed without the consent of the Artifex.
    Please take care to reproduce responsibly so we can keep an eye on our population limit.
    Reproduction Persmissions: please DM Asena on discord to ask for permission

  • Do not cause trouble with other packs.
    You are responsible for your behavior, and the Artifex will not save you from the consequences of your actions. Depending on the level of trouble, it might even be grounds for treason.

    • Ceremonies and Festivals


  • Ceremony of Matrimony - A three day long celebration! The first day begins with two groups for two large hunts beginning at dawn(2-6 characters per thread). The couple is separated and placed in each group as the leader. One group must hunt for the feast, the other group may choose to hunt prey, or collect resources like decorations for the party. Whoever finishes their thread first (3 rounds for each member of the group) wins a Small Accessory (of their choice!)! The first day ends with a small party for the happy couple and the winners. The second day is the ceremony! The Artifex will tie the couple's front paws together with a ribbon or chord under a natural arch of thier choice. Then the couple will speak thier vows over the tie that binds them, then kiss. After the kiss, the feast starts! Allies and friends of Norad are welcome to join the feast. It is customary to bring gifts for the couple and place them in a pile next to the entrance of their home. A bonfire is set in the middle of the village where the revelers can partake in drinking, singing, toasts, feasting, dancing, and stories together well into the third day. The last of the guests to wake the next day is said to be cursed with bad luck for three days! So rise and shine when you can!

  • New life celebration - a celebration to honor the new life brought into the world! It usually takes place a week before a female is due. Games are played, such as a guessing game to guess how many babies will be born. No alcohol will be served here, and males will typically be shooed off. A small lunch is usually served before it's ended. Allies and friends may attend by invitation of the mother-to-be. Gifts are not customary but are welcome for the soon-to-be mother.

  • Craft fair - a market style festival held every summer in the village, all are welcome to display wares in a stall and buy and trade wares from others. Allies and friends are welcome to attend as well as to have their own stall set up. There are usually food stalls and drink stalls as well. The Artifex welcomes and encourages wolves of Norad to display their wares and crafts for our allies to peruse and barter for. Stalls are limited to a large pelts. Depending on how the year has been there may even be excess livestock for trade at the Artifex's home.

      Bellatrix has no knowledge of this pack or their founding.
    • Dragonspire Armada – Neutral
      Bellatrix has yet to meet and form an opinion of their leader. No harm should come to their members unnecessarily.
    • Insomnia – Wary
      Do not harm their members, but do not be afraid to defend yourself with the same energy they bring to the table. Bellatrix has met their leaders at the concord of crowns and has come to learn to be wary of them.
    • Obscura – Neutral
      Bellatrix has met with Scylla, they are neutral to us and we to them. Friendly raiding is encouraged between us.
    • Romalia – Neutral

    • Ryukai – Neutral
      Bellatrix has met their leader and he has refused any form of alliance but has ensured he is a neutral party. Do not cause unnecessary trouble for them or us.
    • The Covenant – Neutral
      Bellatrix has no knowledge of this pack or their Bellatrix was at the founding of this pack, but no trades or anything has been developed. Tread lightly with them.
    • The Syndicate – Tense Neutral
      Do not go out of your way to attack members of their pack, but use good sense when interacting with them. Bellatrix has labeled these wolves as dangerous until proven otherwise. With a change of their leadership known to us now it is unknown if they will continue to be the violent pack they started as.
    • Valhalla – Unknown
      Unknown to us. Do no harm but don't be afraid to defend yourselves. Answer them with the energy they give.

    Ranks

    ArtifexThe leader and militaristic head of Norad. They run things around here and they have final say on things. Their word is law and what they say goes. Their duty is to ensure things run smoothly on the home front and to establish when and where we might need to raid, or when we go to war.
    Bellatrix

    CarbasaThe Diplomatic head of Norad. The secondary alpha of Norad. This is not just a title, and should be respected just as much as the Artifex. They may establish treaties, alliances and declare another pack our enemy, they may not however send us to war without the Artifex agreeing. Their able to promote and demote the same as the Artifex.

    Regium PrimeThe next in line for Artifex. Typically offspring of the Artifex but can be chosen as anyone. This rank cannot be challenged for.
    Sabbath

    ConciliumThe most trusted advisors of Norad. They may make decisions if an Artifex is not around and able to. They may accept recruits. Cannot be challenged for. They are to be double masters in their trades typically and are appointed based on qualifications such as being a previous alpha, being trusted for advice, as well as able to tell their leaders no when there is something they disagree with something. They are typically well travelled, calm and collected under pressure. These are highly respected members of our pack.

    NuptaeThe Artifex and Carbasa's Harem. They are to be respected as much as you do the council and the Artifex and Carbasa. They are the "mothers" of our pack's royalties. They are to ensure our pack is cared for in their own way and defend our borders along side our leaders. This position is not something to snort at, they are respected and often council our leaders along side our concilim. They may not punish unjustly but may demote and promote members they see promise in. This rank cannot be challenged for.

    SwordsThe lead fighters of Norad. The are in charge of making sure disciples who are going to be fighters are progressing. They may hold trainings and are to be at any raid or seige. Can be challenged for by master fighters.

    BowsThe lead hunters of Norad. They are expected to ensure all our food stores are kept up on. They may hold trainings and pack hunts. Can be challenged for by master hunters.

    PestleThe lead healers of Norad. They are expected to ensure the healers of Norad are trained and the herb storage is kept up on. They may hold trainings and are expected to attend all raid and seige healing camps. Can be challenged for by master healers.

    LoomsThe Master craftsmen of Norad. Their main focus is to create new crafts and maintain upkeep on the current crafting good stocks. Can be challenged for by master Intellectuals.

    CouriersThe lead scouts and Navigators of Norad. They are expected to ensure the navigators of Norad are trained and to deliver messages to other packs when the Artifex is unable to. They may hold trainings. Can be challenged for by master Navigators through races.
